Laura is part of the Local Food Hub Family and joined the 4P family after the exciting company merge. Lauren Brown worked for the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) in Washington, D.C. for four years prior to joining Local Food Hub. While at USDA, she held positions in the Office of Congressional Relations, Farm and Foreign Agriculture Services, and with the National Farm to School Program, where she provided technical assistance to schools interested in purchasing locally sourced products, and supported USDA’s $5 million annual Farm to School Grant Program. She moved back to her hometown of Charlottesville, VA, in 2014 to work for Local Food Hub. Laura has worn many hats at Local Food Hub, and currently handles product sales to our customers and contributes to the smooth operating of our distribution efforts. Laura holds a B.A. in Foreign Affairs from the University of Virginia. Laura continues to work from the warehouse outside of Charlottesville, VA.
