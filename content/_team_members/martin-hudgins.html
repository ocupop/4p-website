Martin Hudgins is part of the Local Food Hub Family and joined the 4P family after the exciting company merge. Originally from Chesterfield, Martin grew up around farming and fresh produce. He owned and operated his own landscaping and grading business for several years, then spent 5 years in warehouse management and logistics. From there, he followed his passion for fresh local produce and served as a local buyer for several years, purchasing  local produce, proteins, and dairy across Virginia. He owns and operates a small farm in Cartersville, VA, with both produce and livestock. Martin continues to work from the Ivy warehouse outside of Charlottesville, VA.
