A lifelong vegetable enthusiast, Meg has a passion for food systems change that has been fueled by her time living in Central Appalachia, working with farming communities in Nicaragua, and from developing local food and community health programs as a public health student in St. Louis, Missouri. Most recently, Meg was an Emerson National Hunger Fellow with the Congressional Hunger Center where she worked on food bank program development and nutrition policy. 

Outside of 4P Foods, Meg enjoys distance running, rock climbing, and making the office smell like coffee.
