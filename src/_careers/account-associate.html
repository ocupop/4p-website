About Us
Our vision is to create a new food system that regenerates communities, individuals, and Mother Earth.


MISSION
Our mission at 4P Foods is to connect all people and communities to regional farmers and responsibly sourced food. Our Purpose is to positively impact People, Planet, and Prosperity through a more equitable food system - we stand 4ps and love.
Each day, our work at 4P Foods gets us closer to the kind of food system farmers thrive in, soil is regeneratively cared for, animals are treated humanely, and all communities have equitable access to healthy food.
We achieve this by operating a multi-location food hub, aggregating food from producers throughout the Mid-Atlantic, and delivering it to our customers through our wholesale partnerships, our online grocery store, and our food access programs in underserved communities.


About the role
As a 4P Foods Account Associate, you will ensure customer satisfaction and serve as a critical liaison between customer interest/demand and our procurement team that works closely with our local farmer network. To succeed, you must be committed to providing a positive customer experience through timely, personable and professional communication. Reporting directly to 4P Foods’ Account Manager, your responsibilities will range from daily customer service communication, to managing wholesale accounts, to troubleshooting and problem solving everything from new customer onboarding to continual systems improvement. The ideal candidate is extremely organized and detail-oriented, and is a natural at building relationships with a variety of individuals. Bonus points for experience in food production - the ability to talk shop about our products and farmers is a big part of this role, and the
quicker you can jump into that the better.
This is an exciting opportunity to directly impact the company’s sales operations, and work closely with a variety of teams and team members as well as external partners and 4P Foods members.


Responsibilities
Including but not limited to:

  • Process customer payments and troubleshoot payment issues

  • Provide general administrative support to warehouse staff as needed, including inventory management and related communication
  • Comply with warehouse safety procedures
  • Attend meetings and events within and outside of normal work hours as needed
  • Other duties, as assigned
