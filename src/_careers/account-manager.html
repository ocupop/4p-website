About 4P Foods

Our mission at 4P Foods is to connect all people and communities to regional farmers and responsibly sourced food. Our Purpose is to positively impact People, Planet, and Prosperity through a more equitable food system. Each day, our work at 4P Foods gets us closer to the kind of food system farmers thrive in, soil is regeneratively cared for, animals are treated humanely, and all communities have equitable access to healthy food. 

Since 2014, 4P Foods delivers regenerative and locally-sourced food through its online grocery store, wholesale business operations, and institutional partnerships. With a network of 600 local farmsteads, 4P Foods helps make sustainably grown and ethically raised food accessible to residences, businesses, and school systems across Virginia, Maryland, North Carolina, and Washington, D.C. Each month, 4P Foods delivers nearly 7,500 lbs of food to underserved communities through six community partners.

Position Summary

The Account Manager is responsible for increasing sales of local and regional food across our existing customer base as well acquiring new customers and building long term relationships. They develop sales goals aligned with the organization’s strategic plan, execute, and report on sales achieved and account profitability. The Account Manager works closely with the procurement team to ensure key partner/customer’s needs are met while also ensuring customer needs fit in with internal operations as efficiently as possible. The Account Manager manages their customer base with exceptional customer service through day-to-day activities. The Account Manager are responsible for placing sales orders and managing product availability to ensure growth in basket size per customer whilst ensuring their accounts are profitable. The Account Manager sets weekly prices for the goods ensuring optimum sales are achieved and minimizing waste. Outstanding communication skills and negotiation skills are essential to this position with the desire to build strong working relationships with both their internal and external costumes.

Duties and Responsibilities

The essential functions include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Account Management - Managing the customer relationship including but not limited to relationship building, customer expectations, placing daily orders, and pricing/volume growth models, account profitability.
  • Cross-Department Collaboration - Coordinating solutions on any problems that arise with customers, Procurement, Logistics, Warehouse and other departments. 
  • Reporting Activities - Capturing and presenting key performance indicators to ensure a focus on consistently improving performance.
  • Business Operations - Work with managers and internal customers to understand the company’s needs, understanding how the account manager’s role impacts other departments.
  • Create and develop sales models and reports in order to manage and grow business in order to meet set sales targets.
  • Work with a CRM to ensure Customer accounts are up to date and accurate.
  • Interact with accounts via email, in person, and/or via phone. Cold calling as well as routine meetings with customers is required.
  • Ensure positive relationships, communicate/address issues as they arise, work to find long-term solutions to recurring issues.
  • Evaluate relevant systems. Develop and implement sales procedures as necessary.
  • Assist with market development opportunities (social, promotion, sampling events).
  • Analyze trends and gather sales data to report to Procurement, Operations, Logistics and Management.
  • Coordinate with Procurement and Warehouse managers to determine sale items, conduct product quality assessments, and monitor inventory levels.
  • Performs other related duties as required.
