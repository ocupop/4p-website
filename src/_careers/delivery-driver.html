The Delivery Driver is responsible for aggregating and delivering locally grown and produced farm products to customers in northern Virginia and the surrounding area. The Delivery Driver will ensure that orders are fulfilled in an accurate and timely manner while maintaining strict food safety protocols. The position represents a key link between small regional farms and their communities. Delivery Drivers must be polite and prompt with a commitment to providing excellent customer service to both clients and partner producers.  

Location: â€¯4P Foods has two locations, one in Warrenton, VA and another in Charlottesville, VA. Seeking drivers to work out of both warehouse locations and that can commute to either location, if needed.

JOB DESCRIPTION

  • Pick up, sort, and deliver farm products in and around the Greater DMV and Charlottesville area
  • Clean and prepare delivery vehicles on aggregation and delivery days
  • Understand and comply with relevant food safety policies and regulations and assist with record keeping to maintain requisite documentation
  • Utilize online ordering platform to track deliveries
  • Proactively troubleshoot issues as they arise
  • Attend meetings and events within and outside of normal work hours as needed
  • Act in accordance with 4P Foods mission and uphold the company’s brand and values

REQUIRED SKILLS/EXPERIENCE

  • Valid VA State Driver’s License and clean driving record (must submit driving record upon job offer and be insurable by PCFA auto insurance policy)
  • Must successfully pass DOT physical and receive a DOT medical examiner’s card before start date
  • Ability to drive various vehicles, including Fuso and Izuzu box trucks (CDL NOT required)
  • Ability to work evenings and early mornings
  • Ability to lift and move up to 50 pounds
  • Ability to read, write, and understand English and communicate by email, phone, and texts
  • Ability to accurately follow direction and work in a fast-paced environment
  • Proficient with technology
  • Customer-service oriented and personable demeanor
  • Good organizational skills
  • Sense of humor, flexible, and adaptive personality

PREFERRED SKILLS/EXPERIENCE

  • Prior knowledge of local food systems and sustainable agriculture
  • Work experience on small-scale farm(s) AND/OR in food service (serving/cooking/grocery/etc.)
  • Experience with entrepreneurial and innovative small businesses
  • Delivery driver: 1 year
  • Customer service: 1 year

HOURS AND COMPENSATION

  • 35-40 hours per week, Monday – Friday (overtime may be required)

  • $15.00/hour+

  • Weekly fresh produce box provided
