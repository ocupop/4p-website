Position Summary 

The Local Sales Advocate is responsible for B2B accounts, member satisfaction and to provide general administrative support to warehouse operations. The Local Sales Advocate must be committed to provide a positive customer experience through timely, personable, and professional communication. 

Reporting directly to the Sales Manager, the responsibilities include daily customer service communication, managing wholesale accounts, cultivating new relationships, business opportunities, troubleshooting, problem solving everything from new member on boarding to inventory efficiency. The ideal candidate is extremely organized, detail-oriented and is a natural at building relationships with a variety of individuals. Bonus points for experience in food production - the ability to talk shop about our products and farmers is a big part of this role.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

The essential functions include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Provide general customer support via telephone, email, and in-person.
  • Have superior product knowledge.
  • Own sales inbox and resolve member inquiries in a timely manner.
  • Grow 4P Foods’ wholesale accounts and membership through targeted sale goals, communication and relationship building.
  • Process customer orders from receipt through shipment and delivery, to include processing, tracking, editing, adjustments, and communication.
  • Assist the accounting team in troubleshooting payment issues.
  • Collaborate with warehouse team members to ensure consistency and quality of service.
  • Provide general administrative support to warehouse team members as needed, including inventory management and related communication.
  • Comply with warehouse safety and food safety procedures.
  • Attend meetings and events within and outside of normal work hours as needed.

Minimum Qualifications (Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities)

  • Must be 18  years or older.
  • An Associate’s Degree or high school diploma.
  • Minimum +2 years relevant experience in sales or customer service.
  • Excellent listening and problem-solving skills.
  • The ability to read, write, and perform basic math.
  • Excellent multi-tasking abilities.
  • Ability to proactively anticipate and solve problems; you do not wait for someone to tell you to fix something, you already had a plan in place before it even broke.
  • Exceptional ability to build relationships and work with a diverse group.
  • Strong organization skills with fastidious attention to detail.
  • Must have a good sense of humor.
  • Passionate about building a just and equitable food system in the United States.

Physical Demands and Work Environment 

  • Maintain a positive attitude and focus on member satisfaction in a fast-paced and changing environment to meet tight deadlines.
  • Ability to sit, stand and walk for extended periods of time.
  • Flexible work schedule (which may include nights, weekends, holidays, and long hours) and regular attendance is necessary.
  • Ability to lift 30 pounds or more without assistance.
