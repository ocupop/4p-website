Job Summary:

The Logistics & Warehouse Operations Assistant is responsible for shipment planning, routing, customer communication, database management, reporting and administrative tasks around the fleet and warehouse operations. This role reports to the Logistics Director and will play a proactive, essential role in improving the logistics of 4P.

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Coordinate with in-house teams to execute accurate and timely processing of orders and deliveries.
  • Maintain Vendor and Customer Database
  • Communicate with Vendors and Customers in a timely manner.
  • Track and report Vehicle Maintenance needs and report on mileage and other costs.
  • Ensure Maintenance logs for fleet and Warehousing needs are up to date at all times.
  • Manage contractors and correct billing based on contract terms.
  • Ensure paperwork is accurate and filed daily.
  • Support with maintaining logistics policies, procedures, support plans, and reporting needs.
  • Monitor and track temperatures or WH and Fleet daily.
  • Creates and maintains various reports and overviews to track the performance of a team.
  • Anticipate and identify problems and develop a plan to implement solutions in an unbiased, logical manner.
  • Performs other related duties as required.
