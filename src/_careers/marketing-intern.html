About 4P Foods

Our mission at 4P Foods is to connect all people and communities to regional farmers and responsibly sourced food. Our Purpose is to positively impact People, Planet, and Prosperity through a more equitable food system. Each day, our work at 4P Foods gets us closer to the kind of food system farmers thrive in, soil is regeneratively cared for, animals are treated humanely, and all communities have equitable access to healthy food. 

Since 2014, 4P Foods delivers regenerative and locally-sourced food through its online grocery store, wholesale business operations, and institutional partnerships. With a network of 600 local farmsteads, 4P Foods helps make sustainably grown and ethically raised food accessible to residences, businesses, and school systems across Virginia, Maryland, North Carolina, and Washington, D.C. Each month, 4P Foods delivers nearly 7,500 lbs of food to underserved communities through six community partners.

Position Summary

The Marketing Intern will help the Marketing Team tell the story of our producers and farmers and maintain the source transparency of 4P Foods products. This position will allow you to improve your research, interviewing, copywriting, and graphic design skills. There is also the opportunity to manage a social media campaign and host an Instagram takeover on a farm!

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

The essential functions include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Interviews producers and farmers for producer profiles.
  • Writes content for producer profiles.
  • Collects images and edit images for producer profiles.
  • Writes content for product descriptions.
  • Collects images and edit images for products.
  • Creates social media campaigns around producer/farmer interviews.
  • Visits farms to collect images, video, and interview farmers. 
  • Hosts an Instagram takeover on the farm.
  • Works with the marketing team on identifying areas of improvement, implementing processes, and documenting them accordingly.

 Minimum Qualifications (Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities

  • Eagerness to grow personally and professionally.
  • Proficient with social media platforms including Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn.
  • Demonstrated interviewing skills.
  • Demonstrated content writing skills.
  • Proficient in graphic design and familiarity with Canva.
  • Ability to organize tasks and meet deadlines.
  • Must have strong interpersonal skills and be capable of communicating with a diverse range of individuals.
  • Excellent tech skills including working knowledge of Google Suite products (Docs, Sheets, Slides) as well as Zoom, Google Meetup, Slack. Ability to easily learn and use content management system for food database and project management system.
  • Demonstrated professional demeanor.

 Physical Demands and Work Environment

  • Ability to work in various temperatures.
  • Ability to travel to farms and warehouse locations.
  • Ability to stay alert, on your feet, and in constant motion for most of the shift.
  • Ability to twist, bend, sit, and stand for extended periods of time.
