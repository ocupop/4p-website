We are excited to announce the launch of our 2024 Wholesale Earth Day Awards Challenge! This is an opportunity for us to come together and recognize the outstanding efforts of our partners who are making a positive impact on our regional food system and supporting independently-owned farms and businesses.

At 4P Foods, we believe that every choice we make about the food we eat has the power to change the world. By supporting small farms, we are not only nourishing ourselves but also investing in the health of our environment, our local economies, and the future generations to come.

To honor and celebrate your dedication, we have created several award categories that showcase the different ways our community contributes to building a better food system. These award categories include:

Support of Small Farms

This award recognizes individuals or businesses who consistently prioritize supporting small farms in our region. By choosing to source from these farms, you are directly contributing to the preservation of sustainable agricultural practices and the livelihoods of small farmers.

Order Deadline Champion

This award goes to those who always ensure that their orders are placed on time, allowing us to fulfill them efficiently. Your commitment and punctuality play a crucial role in enabling us to deliver fresh, regionally sourced products to your doorstep.

By embracing the changing seasons and enjoying a diverse range of fruits and vegetables, you are not only supporting regenerative farming practices but also promoting a healthier and more sustainable food culture.

Bulk Product Sales

This award is for those who make a significant impact through their bulk product orders. By purchasing in large quantities, you are helping to create a stable market for farmers and reducing food waste through efficient distribution.
