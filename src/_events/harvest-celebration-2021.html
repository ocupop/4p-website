Celebrate autumn with 4P Foods on Saturday, October 23! Join us at Messick’s Farm Market to enjoy a wonderful fall day and all that the Farm Market has to offer. 4P will be set up outside of the festival area to connect with the 4P community. Stop by for a chat any time between 11a.m. to 3p.m. and to enjoy the harvest festival put on by our farm partner in Bealeton, VA. Messick’s Fall Festival includes hay rides, a pumpkin patch, a corn maze, live music, and more!
