In this panel discussion you will hear from diverse farmers across Virginia on how they are implementing soil health practices on their operation and what inspires them to be stewards of the land. The event will be live on Facebook.

This panel discussion is part of 4 the Soil's celebration of World Soil Day. The United Nations set up World Soil Day to raise awareness about the importance of healthy soil and sustainable soil management practices. World Soil Day is on Sunday, December 5.
