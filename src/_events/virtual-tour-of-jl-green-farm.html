What does regenerative agriculture look like? Join the 4P team as we talk with farmers Jordan and Laura Green to find out!

J & L Green Farm is a grass-based sustainable farm and one of the largest outdoor pasture-based farrow to finish farms in the state of Virginia. Tune in to see the 200-acre farm in Edinburg, Virginia and learn what makes their pork and humane farming methods so special. J & L is a partnering farm and source of pastured and forested pork and grass-fed beef which is available for delivery in any 4P Protein Bag.

Jordan and Laura agreed to host a Virtual Tour with the 4P community one sunny day, which is available for viewing on our Facebook page. It was so inspiring to learn about their farm and practices and despite a brief interlude, the virtual event is available for streaming in two parts below! Read more about J & L Green Farm here.
