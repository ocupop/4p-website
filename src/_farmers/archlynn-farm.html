Archlynn Farm is a multi-generation family owned and operated produce farm in Charlotte County, Va. They grow produce year-round. 

You can find Archlynn produce in your 4P Foods produce tote, Lynchburg farmers market, Farmville farmers market, and at their farm stand on Archlynn Farm.

They also have pick-your-own strawberries from May to early June or while the crop lasts.
