Established in 2001, Barajas Produce is a sustainable farm in Montross, Virginia that was started on 1 acre of land. The Barajas family now farms 110 acres. They grow a variety of crops including cabbage, beets, rainbow chard, strawberries, cauliflower, and so much more.

Their primary goal is to use the best farming practices and to take great care of the land. As they grow and acquire new customers, they are diversifying their crops and incorporating new sustainable practices to their operation.
