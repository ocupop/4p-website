Farmstead Ferments Fermentaria & Mercantile is located in Scottsville, VA. Since 2010, they have been growing, processing, and fermenting a wide variety of vegetables, fruits, and herbs into tasty, nutrient-dense foods that support the health of the local Virginia community. 

Passionate about promoting a homestead-based lifestyle with their raw and naturally-fermented products, they aim to enhance digestion, boost immunity, and provide delicious party-on-the-plate experiences. Their products' flavors draw inspiration from the local and seasonal abundance, incorporating ingredients sourced from their own farm, Free Bird Farm. Their fermented foods include sauerkrauts, kimchi, pickles, chutneys, and salsas that are rich in vitamins, minerals, probiotics, enzymes, and electrolytes. 

The Farmstead Mercantile stands as a retail storefront for Farmstead Ferments products. Here you can sample a wide variety of  ever-changing kombucha flavors. And there's more - the Mercantile Apothecary offers over a hundred varieties of organic herbs, spices, teas, and herbal body products. For those who love getting hands-on, Farmstead Ferments provide DIY fermentation starter cultures and supplies!
