Homestead Creamery is a small and locally-owned dairy processing plant located in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Virginia.

Opening its doors in 2001, Homestead Creamery was the dream of two small dairy farmers that wanted to preserve their farms for future generations.

Today, Homestead Creamery processes dairy for a network of small, family-owned dairies who dedicated to the health and happiness of their cows. Diary farms that process their product at Homestead Creamery include:

  • Stonebrook Dairy in Wirtz, Va.
  • Goldenview Dairy in Glade Hill, Va.
  • Redvale Dairy in Boones Mill, Va.
  • Whispering Oak Farm in Rocky Mount, Va.
  • Riverbend Dairy in Rocky Mount, Va.
  • Homeland Jerseys in Wirtz, Va.
