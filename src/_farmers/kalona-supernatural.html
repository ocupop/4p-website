Kalona SuperNatural is a small farming community in the heartland of America. They work with small, sustainable family farms to provide delicious, certified organic whole milk and dairy products from regeneratively-managed, grass-fed cows. 

Kalona SuperNatural believes in going beyond the organic practices required by the government– for the health of animals, the planet and your family. 

“Our herd is free-range and pastures year-round, rejuvenating the soil, water, and air. Their movements create healthier milk with a natural goodness that we protect through minimal processing in our creamery”, says Kalona SuperNatural. 

Kalona SuperNatural’s farmer partners are working toward a sustainable future by implementing the Savory Institute’s Ecological Outcome Verification™ (EOV) monitoring protocol.
