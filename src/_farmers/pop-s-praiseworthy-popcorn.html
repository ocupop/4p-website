Pop's Praiseworthy Popcorn is a woman-owned business in Sterling, Va. Pop's Praiseworthy Popcorn brings joy to the moment! Pop's is whole grain, non-GMO, vegan and gluten free. Add to that a bit of sweetness and a touch of salt and you've got a scrumptious treat. Each bag is filled to the brim with actual popcorn, not air!

At Pop's, their commitment to quality is at the heart of everything they do. They take pride in crafting artisanal flavors that are uniquely infused with the finest ingredients, ensuring that every bite of their popcorn is a culinary masterpiece. Each flavor in their lineup is a testament to their dedication to providing you with a popcorn experience like no other. The popcorn journey begins with the careful selection of kernels that are specially grown for their size and shape. This meticulous attention to detail ensures that you receive popcorn with the perfect texture and crunch in every bag.
