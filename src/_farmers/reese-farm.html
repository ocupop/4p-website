Reese Farm is located in Halifax County. It is owned and operated by the Reese family, and has been a partner of Local Food Hub since 2014. A GAP (Good Agricultural Practices) certified farm, Reese Farm grows and sells vegetables, strawberries, cantaloupe, watermelon, and sweet corn. In addition to produce, Reese Farm utilizes greenhouses on site to propagate bedding plants for sale, and in the late season they offer mums, pumpkins, and holiday trees and custom wreaths made from greenery from the farm.

Reese Farm is a is a Local Food Hub partner farm. We are proud to partner with Local Food Hub to connect small family farmers in Virginia to 4P FOODS customers.
