Rob Glover grew up working on the family farm in Bailey, NC with his father and uncle. He found a passion for agriculture and after graduating from high school he started his own farming business. Rob continues to learn more about agriculture while participating in many programs through NC State, the most recent being the NC Tobacco Trust Fund Commission Agricultural Leadership Development Program.

Rob Glover Farming grows many produce crops including strawberries, broccoli, squash, cucumbers, watermelon, cantaloupe, and honeydew. Rob and his wife, Kaitlin, opened Rob's Fresh Produce Market in 2020 as a way to bring more fresh produce to their community.
