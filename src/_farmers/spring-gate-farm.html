Goat meat is the most consumed meat in the world and provides a lean alternative to other red meats. The North American Cashmere Goat (NACG) is a dual- purpose animal, providing both fiber and meat products. 

Owner of Spring Gate Farm, Jane Hammond has raised NACGs for over 20 years. The farm offers a full range of products including, breeding stock, companion/pets, pelts, meat and fiber. Spring Gate Farm practices sustainable agriculture which protects and preserves the land as well as the animals. Jane's picturesque 220-acre farm is home to her prized herd of Cashmere Goats, a breed known for its luxuriously soft, fine fiber—cashmere. Her one-of-a-kind herd has won honors in both fiber and confirmation competitions, both in Canada and the U.S.

We invite you to try the delicious goat products from Spring Gate Farm in our shop.
