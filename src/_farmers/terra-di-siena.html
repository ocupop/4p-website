At 4P Foods, we believe food is more than sustenance—it's a story of land, tradition, and community. Terra di Siena USA embodies this ethos, bringing the rich heritage of Tuscan salumi to the heart of Virginia.

A Legacy Rooted in Tradition

The Gambassi family's dedication to artisanal meat curing dates back over 120 years in San Gimignano, Italy. Filippo Gambassi, the fifth-generation artisan, continues this legacy in the U.S., producing authentic Italian cured meats using time-honored recipes and methods.
