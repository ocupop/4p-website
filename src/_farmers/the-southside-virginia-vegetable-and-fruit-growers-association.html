Southside, or Southside Virginia, has traditionally referred to the portion of the state south of the James River. The farmers that make up the association are located in this area of the state.

The Southside Virginia Vegetable and Fruit Grower's Association is an organization that promotes and encourages improvements in produce and value-added products grown in Southside Virginia. The association works to develop partnerships that improve the production and marketing of local produce and support the community.
