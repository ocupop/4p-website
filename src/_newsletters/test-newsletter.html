April 18th, 19th & 20th

Good afternoon ,

EXCITING NEWS: Our new website is coming soon! We're pleased to share a new and improved shopping experience with you in just a few weeks. You will access more information about where your food comes from, better layout, and even more products. Until then, you'll notice our categories have changed as we prepare for the new site. Use the search function or contact us with any questions. We're here to help!

In your next box you'll find, Little Sweetie mini cantaloupe, yellow squash, red cabbage, sliced tomatoes, bananas, and more. You are sure to enjoy some of the many tastes of summer!

Browse our Pinterest Page and Recipe Blog for recipes. Love to cook? Tag @shenandoahproduce in your photos for a chance to be featured on our page!

In these unprecedented times, we are so grateful for your support and for your choice to eat healthy, fresh food from our trusted network of small farms and artisans. Please read our COVID-19 response statement here.

NEW: MEAT SUBSCRIPTION

You've asked and we've listened! New farm box type is the Meat Share, a rotating selection of grass-fed, non-gm, sustainably sourced meat. Each week, we hand-pick four products. Keep our selection or mix and match from a larger list to suit your household needs. We've partnered with our food hub partner, 4P Foods to deliver healthy meat products. All items will arrive frozen packed in an insulated re-usable box and tote bag which can be left outside the following week to return to your driver.

Pictured: Ground Beef, Grass-Fed from J&L Green Farm in Edinburg, Virginia. See all meat items in this week's Meat Share on our website. Tip: As with all subscriptions, you have until midnight on Monday PM to customize your order!
