--- title: VDOE Connects Schools to Virginia Food Through Pilot Procurement Program press_date: 2022-04-18 00:00:00 description: >- The Virginia Department of Education is partnering with regional food hubs to deliver nearly $2.2 million worth of locally produced and grown food products to school nutrition programs in the commonwealth. The Centralized Local Procurement Pilot Program — funded by a U.S. Department of Agriculture Supply Chain Assistance grant — is designed to help school nutrition programs avoid supply chain disruptions and strengthen regional food systems. source: Virginia Department of Education author: Jillian Balow, Superintendent of Public Instruction featured_image: /uploads/new-media-release-header-govdelivery-rev2-01-crop.png link: https://content.govdelivery.com/accounts/VADOE/bulletins/313f47d ---
