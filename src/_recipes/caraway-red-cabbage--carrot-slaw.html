--- layout: recipe title: Quick Caraway Red Cabbage and Carrot Coleslaw Side Dish Recipe author: Red Root & Co. description_html: >-

If you're not a fan of coleslaw, you will be after trying this recipe. You’ll be saying, “I fought the slaw and the slaw won.” This delicious, refreshing vegetarian side dish is quick to make and serves up to ten people. The perfect addition to your menu for an autumn barbeque or a winter holiday party. The caraway red cabbage and the orange carrots give this garnish a bright and festive color. Simply add all of the ingredient to a mixing bowl and store in the refrigerator until you’re ready to impress your guests. Trust us, the slaw will win over your taste buds. 

ingredients_html: >-
  • 1 head of red cabbage, shredded
  • 3 large carrots, shredded
  • 1 small anaheim pepper, chopped or a 1 TBS of chopped jalapeno, if you want heat
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ¼ cup Red Root & Co. Heirloom Garlic Oxymel 
  • ¼ cup cold pressed olive oil 
  • 1 TBS caraway seeds
  • 1-1 ½ tsp salt 
  • 1 cup parsley, chopped
directions_html: >-

Combine all of the ingredients into a large mixing bowl. Store in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
