--- layout: recipe title: Caraway Red Cabbage & Carrot Slaw author: Red Root & Co. description_html: >-

Crunchy, colorful, and delicious, this is a flavorful addition to a weekend picnic lunch. Use the leftovers as a condiment, salad topping, and side dish throughout the week. 

ingredients_html: >-

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 head of red cabbage, shredded
  • 3 large carrots, shredded
  • 1 small anaheim pepper, chopped or a 1 TBS of chopped jalapeno, if you want heat
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ¼ cup RR&Co Heirloom Garlic Oxymel 
  • ¼ cup cold pressed olive oil 
  • 1 TBS caraway seeds
  • 1-1 ½ tsp salt 
  • 1 cup parsley, chopped
