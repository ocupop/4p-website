--- layout: recipe title: Easy Frozen Custard Dessert Recipe author: 4P Foods Team Member description_html: >-

You dessert the best and easiest frozen custard recipe out there, and 4P Foods and Homestead Creamery can provide that recipe! This recipe is so simple and makes a great dessert or snack for a holiday party. Top with locally-made chocolates, delicious chocolate covered peanuts, or organic pomegranate seeds. All you need for this recipe is a container that can go in the freezer (really, that’s it!). Freezing Homestead Creamery’s custard will create a delicious vanilla flavored dessert with a milkshake like texture.

ingredients_html: >-
  • 1 quart Homestead Creamery custard
  • Pomegranate seeds, optional
  • Chocolate covered peanuts, optional
  • Chocolate covered coffee beans, optional
  • Chocolate almond clusters
directions_html: >-

Transfer your Homestead Creamery custard to a freezer safe container. Freeze for 24 hours.

Scoop into a bowl of your choice and top with all of your favorite toppings. Enjoy!
