This makes a single serving and is great all year round, but is especially good for school lunches.

ingredients_html: >-
  • 2 tbsp. almond butter
  • 1 tbsp. concord grape jam
  • 1 Greek style pita
directions_html: >-

Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Warm your pita in the oven for 3-4 minutes or until it has reached your desired temperature.

Using a butter knife spread the almond butter on top of the pita in an even layer. Then, on top of the almond butter, place three small dollops of concord grape jam. Use the tip of the butter knife to swirl the jam into the almond butter. Enjoy!
