--- layout: recipe title: Fast Almond Butter & Jam Pita Weekday Lunch Recipe author: 4P Foods Team Member description_html: >-

Nostalgia on a plate? That’s our jam. This fast and easy recipe will take five minutes and is perfect for lunch, a quick weeknight dinner, or just a snack. Heirloom almond butter for Big Spoon Roasters and locally grown jam elevate this take on a PB&J. This makes a single serving and is great all year round, but is especially good for school lunches.

ingredients_html: >-

  • 2 tbsp. almond butter

  • 1 tbsp. jam of your choice

  • 1 Greek style pita

directions_html: >-

Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Warm your pita in the oven for 3-4 minutes or until it has reached your desired temperature.

Using a butter knife spread the almond butter on top of the pita in an even layer. Then, on top of the almond butter, place three small dollops of jam. Use the tip of the butter knife to swirl the jam into the almond butter. Enjoy!
