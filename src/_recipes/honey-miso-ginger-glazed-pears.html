--- layout: recipe title: Honey, Miso, Ginger Glazed Pears author: Annaliegh Baremore, Local Food Sales Advocate description_html: >-

These baked pears make a great addition to a roast chicken, your morning oatmeal, or a quick salad at lunch. You could even just eat them as a sweet and savory snack!

ingredients_html: >-
  • 1 large asian pear (two small pears will also do) 
  • 2 tbsp honey 
  • 1 tbsp grated or minced ginger
  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil (or other neutral oil) 
  • 1 tbsp red miso paste 
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • A bit of butter 
directions_html: >-

Start by chopping the pear into ¼ inch thin slices. Set aside. In a small bowl, combine the miso paste, sunflower oil, honey, and balsamic vinegar and stir until thoroughly combined. Add the pear slices and toss to evenly distribute the wet ingredients. Lightly grease a baking sheet with butter, and then lay the coated pear slices on the sheet. Bake at 425 degrees for about 10 minutes or until golden brown.
