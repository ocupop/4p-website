--- layout: recipe title: Quick Honey, Miso, and Ginger Glazed Pear Snack Recipe author: 4P Foods Team Member description_html: >-

A quick and simple way to enjoy in-season pears this autumn. This recipe works with Virginia-grown Asian pears or regionally-sourced Bartlett  or bosc pears, and it makes a tasty snack or great garnish. Honey, miso, and ginger glazed pears pair pear-fectly with your morning oatmeal, a roast chicken, or salad greens. Get creative with this vegetarian recipe and make it your own. The measurements listed below make two to three servings.

ingredients_html: >-
  • 1 large asian pear or 2 bartlett or bosc pears
  • 2 tbsp honey 
  • 1 tbsp grated or minced ginger
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp red miso paste 
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp. butter
directions_html: >-

Preheat the oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit. 

Slice the pear into ¼ inch thin slices. Set aside. 

In a small bowl, combine the miso paste, oil, honey, and balsamic vinegar and stir until thoroughly combined. Add the pear slices and toss to evenly distribute the wet ingredients.

Lightly grease a baking sheet with butter, and then lay the coated pear slices on the sheet. 

Bake at 425 degrees for about 10 minutes or until golden brown.
