--- layout: recipe title: Hot Wings & Blue Cheese Dip author: Julianne Hart, Marketing Manager description_html: >-

These wings are for sharing with friends! Julianne cooks the wings in an air-fryer on the grill pan, but the wings could also be done on a grill. The blue cheese dip is her dad's recipe. "The blue cheese dip says 'black pepper to taste', because my dad adds A LOT of black pepper. Like too much," Julianne explained. 

ingredients_html: >-

Hot Wings

  • 1.25lbs of chicken wings
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 1/4 cup chipotle hot sauce
  • 1/8 cup siracha hot sauce

Blue Cheese Dip

  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled blue cheese 
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon celery salt
  • Black pepper to taste
directions_html: >-

Hot Wings

Pat wings dry and season with salt and pepper. Place wings on the grill pan for the air-fryer. Use the air fry/grill setting on the air-fryer. Cook the wings for 20 minutes at 360 degrees. Make sure the wings reach an internal temp of 165 degrees. Wisk together melted butter, siracha hot sauce, and chipotle hot sauce. Toss wings in the sauce.

Blue Cheese Dip

Combine mayonnaise, sour cream, garlic powder, celery salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Then, fold in the crumbled blue cheese.
