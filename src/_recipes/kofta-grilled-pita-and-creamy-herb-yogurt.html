Kofta

  • 1 lb. of ground pork, beef or lamb
  • 1 tsp. salt 
  • 1 tsp. ground pepper
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin 
  • 1/4 tsp. ground chile de árbol (can substitute with ground cayenne pepper)
  • 1/4 tsp. paprika 
  • 1/4 tsp. finely chopped rosemary
  • 1/4 tsp. finely chopped thyme 
  • 1 tbsp. red wine

Yogurt 

  • 1/2 cup greek-style yogurt
  • 1 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp. Extra Virgin Olive Oil 2 tbsp. water
  • 1/2 tbsp. chopped parsley 1/2 tbsp. chopped cilantro 1/2 tbsp. chopped dill
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. local honey
  • dash of hot sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste 
directions_html: >-

Yogurt: Combine all ingredients and whisk. 
 

Kofta: Combine meat and spices. Refrigerate for 1/2 hour. Shape into four 4 oz. tube-shaped patties and grill until an internal temperature of 160 ÌŠF is reached. Grill or toast pita. 

Serve with yogurt sauce, sliced red onion, radish, cilantro leaves and your favorite pickles! 

4 servings
