--- layout: recipe title: Organic Hummus, Lettuce, & Tomato Sandwich Lunch Recipe author: 4P Foods Team Member description_html: >-

A sandwich to hum-muse your taste buds. This recipe calls for Little Sesame Hummus made from regenerative organic chickpeas! Completely vegetarian, this sandwich is a good meatless alternative for a BLT and with Little Sesame’s different flavors of hummus you can really make this to your liking. This sandwich is a simple summertime lunch that you’ll keep coming back to.

ingredients_html: >-
  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 leaves of bibb lettuce
  • 2 tbsp. Little Sesame hummus of your choice
directions_html: >-

Simply spread your hummus on the sourdough bread. Layer on your lettuce and tomato and top it off with the last slice of bread. Cut your sandwich in half and enjoy!
