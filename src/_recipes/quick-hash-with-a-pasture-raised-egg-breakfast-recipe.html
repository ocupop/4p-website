--- layout: recipe title: Quick Hash with a Pasture-Raised Egg Breakfast Recipe author: 4P Foods Team Member description_html: >-

In a rush and need a meal quickly? Let’s hash it out. Stone’s Throw Hash is a great base for a quick meal. This premade, frozen dish only needs to be heated up, and then you add a pasture-raised egg for protein!

This quick and easy recipe serves 1-2 people and all you need is a frying pan.

ingredients_html: >-

1 pack of Stone’s Throw Hash

1-2 farm fresh egg

directions_html: >-

Warm a frying pan over medium heat. Add the Stone’s Throw Hash to the frying pan. Allow to defrost.

Once your hash is at your desired temperature, clear a space in the pan to fry your pasture-raised eggs.

Serve hash on a plate with the fried egg on top.
