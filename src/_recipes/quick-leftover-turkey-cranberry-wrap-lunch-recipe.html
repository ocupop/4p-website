--- layout: recipe title: Quick Leftover Turkey & Cranberry Wrap Lunch Recipe author: 4P Foods Team Member description_html: >-

Use organic Ula Tortilla to wrap up your Thanksgiving leftovers. This quick lunch recipe is a great way to use up leftover cranberry sauce, turkey, lettuce mix, and cheese. This is also a great way to wrap up autumn flavors into an easy portable lunch for one.

ingredients_html: >-

1 Ula tortilla

Shredded turkey

1 tbsp. cranberry sauce

Lettuce mix

Your favorite cheese

directions_html: >-

Steam or toast your Ula tortilla depending on your preference. Place turkey, cranberry sauce, cheese, and lettuce mix on the tortilla and roll the tortilla up.
