With vanilla yogurt and locally-made jam, this quick recipe will be your go to for breakfast and snacks year round. You could say it’ll be your jam! Pequea Valley Vanilla Yogurt is perfect for layering just add in your favorite granola, nuts, or fruit to this vegetarian recipe.

ingredients_html: >-

  • 1 6oz Pequea Valley Vanilla Yogurt

  • 3 tsp. Jam (or more if you want)

  • Granola

  • Fruit, optional

  • Nuts, optional

directions_html: >-

Start with a clean jar and put a layer of granola in the bottom. Then, spoon in 2oz of vanilla yogurt. On top of the yogurt, add 1 tsp. of jam. Then, add a layer of fruit, nuts, or more granola. Keep layering until you use up all of the vanilla yogurt.
