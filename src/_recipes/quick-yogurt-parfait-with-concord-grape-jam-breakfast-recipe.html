Wenger Grape Farm Concord Grape Jam and Pequea Valley Vanilla Yogurt are perfect for layering from there just add in your favorite granola, nuts, or fruit to this vegetarian recipe.

ingredients_html: >-
  • 1 6oz Pequea Valley Vanilla Yogurt
  • 3 tsp. Concord Grape Jam (or more if you want)
  • Granola
  • Fruit, optional
  • Nuts, optional
directions_html: >-

Start with a clean jar and put a layer of granola in the bottom. Then, spoon in 2oz of vanilla yogurt. On top of the yogurt, add 1 tsp. of grape jam. Then, add a layer of fruit, nuts, or more granola. Keep layering until you use up all of the vanilla yogurt.
