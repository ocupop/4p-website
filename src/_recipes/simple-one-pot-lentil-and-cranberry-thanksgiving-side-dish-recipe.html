--- layout: recipe title: Simple One-Pot Lentil and Cranberry Thanksgiving Side Dish Recipe author: Bonnie May, 4P Foods Team Member description_html: >-

Live a lentil this autumn with delicious lentils, cranberries, and peak-season vegetables. You only need one-pot to create this lentil and cranberry Thanksgiving side dish for your next fall gathering. This recipe serves 4-6 people and would make a delicious, lite vegetarian lunch.

ingredients_html: >-
  • ½ cup cooked cranberries
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • ½ cup peas 
  • 1 cups lentils 
  • ½ cup carrots
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 teaspoons olive oil
  • Salt 
  • Pepper
  • Fresh or dried parsley 
  • Fresh or dried thyme 
  • Roasted walnuts (optional) 
  • Feta cheese or goat cheese (optional) 
  • Drizzle of honey (optional)
directions_html: >-

In a medium size pot on medium heat, saute the chopped onion and garlic in olive oil for 5 minutes. 

Add water and lentils to the pot. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.

Add peas, carrots, bay leaf, parsley, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir to combine. Allow to simmer for 15-20 minutes. 

Once cooked, remove the bay leaves. Add in lemon juice and cooked cranberries. Stir to combine. Taste and see if you need more salt, pepper or lemon juice. Garnish with walnuts, goat cheese, feta cheese, or drizzle with local honey.
