--- layout: recipe title: Simple Spinach And Strawberry Smoothie Breakfast Recipe author: 4P Foods Team Member description_html: >-

Spring breakfast made easy and delicious. This spinach and strawberry smoothie recipe makes two smoothies and is the perfect addition to brunch, morning walk, or your mid-afternoon snack. This recipe makes it easy to get your greens and  is vegetarian. It could be made vegan by swapping out the dairy items.

ingredients_html: >-
  • 2 cups spinach, frozen
  • 1 medium banana, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup strawberries, frozen
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup vanilla yogurt
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 ½ tbsp. maple syrup
directions_html: >-

Freeze strawberries and spinach overnight.

Add frozen strawberries, spinach, banana, yogurt, and ice to a blender. Pour milk and maple syrup over top of the solid ingredients. Blend until smooth. 

Pour into 2 glasses. Serve immediately.
