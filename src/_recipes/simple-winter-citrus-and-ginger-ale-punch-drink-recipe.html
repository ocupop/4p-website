--- layout: recipe title: Simple Winter Citrus and Ginger Ale Punch Drink Recipe author: 4P Foods Team Member description_html: >-

This citrus-ly fun drink is bubbly and festive. Our selection of organic winter citrus combined with locally made ginger ale makes a wonderfully refreshing, colorful, and flavorful drink for holiday parties or winter gatherings. Plus, this drink has immune boosting Vitamin C and gut soothing ginger ale for the health conscious party guest. This drink recipe serves four to five people, but can easily be scaled up for larger groups.

ingredients_html: >-
  • 24 fl. oz. ginger ale, chilled
  • 3 oranges, juiced
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • ½ orange, thinly sliced
  • 1 lime, thinly sliced
  • Vodka of your choice (if you want to make it an adult beverage)
directions_html: >-

In a large pitcher or bowl, juice your three oranges and half of the lime. 

If you’re adding vodka in, pour in your desired amount and stir.

Pour your ginger ale into the pitcher or bowl. Gently stir.

Garnish with your thinly sliced orange and lime. 

Pour into glasses and enjoy!
