--- layout: recipe title: Smoky Grilled Turnips w/ Lemon Thyme Aioli author: Cooking & Drinking description_html: ingredients_html: >-

Turnips 

  • 3 medium turnips
  • 2 tbsp. Extra Virgin Olive Oil 
  • generous pinch of smoked paprika 
  • salt and pepper to taste

Aioli

  • 1 large farm fresh egg
  • 1 clove garlic 
  • 1 + 1/2 tbsp. lemon juice 
  • zest of one lemon 
  • 1/2 tsp. Dijon mustard 
  • 1/2 tsp. finely chopped thyme leaves 
  • 1 cup vegetable or grape seed oil
directions_html: >-

Aioli: Place all ingredients, except oil, into blender then slowly add oil while blending, allowing the ingredients to emulsify into a smooth homogenous sauce. This is a great job for your handheld immersion blender or small to medium-sized blender. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 

Turnips: Wash and peel. Cut into 1/4” disks. Toss in olive oil, salt, pepper, smoked paprika. Grill on high, 2-4 minutes on each side until tender. Alternatively, cut into 1” chunks and skewer to make kebabs. This recipe is versatile — try it with radishes!
 

4 servings
