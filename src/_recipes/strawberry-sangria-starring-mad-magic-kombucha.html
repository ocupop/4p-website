When our friends at Mad Magic Kombucha shared this recipe for Strawberry Sangria spiked with their locally brewed kombucha, we knew that it was a cocktail that would charm your tastebuds too! Refreshing strawberry sangria made on the spot with oranges, strawberries, and a kombucha flair. Made with just 7 ingredients that are easy to throw together in less than 10 minutes. Perfect for outdoor summer entertaining. Cheers!

ingredients_html: >-
  • ...Dry Spanish Red Wine (I used Tempranillo!), chilled
  • Orange, sliced, seeds removed and peeled left on 
  • Strawberries
  • Orange juice 
  • Mad Magic Strawberry Kombucha (or preferred flavor)
  • Maple syrup, to taste
  • Ice, for serving
directions_html: >-

Fruit: Add the diced strawberries and the unpeeled orange slices to a large mixing bowl or pitcher and muddle them with a muddler for about a minute. If you don't have a muddler, not to worry! Get creative with a substitute kitchen tool to smash the orange slices against the bottom of a tall glass, like a fork.

Add in the orange juice and Mad Magic Strawberry Kombucha and muddle everything again to combine for about 30 seconds. 

Kombucha: This flavor enhances the strawberry vibe while adding powerful probiotics to your happy hour cheers. We added 1/4 cup in this recipe but you can add up to 1/2 cup for more flavor!

Wine: Add your chilled bottle of dry red wine and stir to incorporate. Choose an inexpensive, dry bottle for this recipe. Mix all ingredients together and chill until ready to enjoy. Serve in a cup with ice and fruit garnish.

Tips for adjusting the flavor to taste: 

  • Add more Strawberry Kombucha, strawberries, orange, orange juice to achieve the balance of fruit and sweet. 
  • For sweet sangria, add 1-2 tablespoons of maple syrup for sweeter sangria.
