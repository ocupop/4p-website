--- layout: recipe title: Quick Strawberry Sherry Cobbler Cocktail Recipe author: Red Root & Co. description_html: >-

If you're looking for the berry best strawberry cocktail for your next party, gathering, barbeque, or picnic this recipe is for you. Enjoy the delicious flavors of organic orange and lemon slices paired with Red Root & Co. Strawberry Rose Geranium Shrub. The subtle citrus flavor highlights the sweetness of the fruit and makes this drink just perfect for a warm afternoon, happy hour in the sun, or holiday party. 

You will need a muddler or another kitchen tool to smash the orange and lemon against the bottom of a glass and a cocktail shaker to ensure the drink is mixed well. With just a few ingredients, this quick recipe will have you enjoying the berry best drink in no time.

ingredients_html: >-
  • 1 generous orange wheel
  • 1 lemon wheel
  • ¾ oz RR&Co Strawberry Rose Geranium Shrub
  • 3 oz amontillado sherry, such as Lustau
  • 1 tsp agave syrup, honey or simple syrup, optional*
  • Citrus, berries, or mint for garnish
directions_html: >-

1. Add the lemon, orange, RR&Co Strawberry Rose Geranium Shrub and sweetener to a cocktail shaker and muddle well. Add the sherry, fill with ice, and shake. Finely strain into a narrow tumbler over crushed ice. Top up with additional crushed ice, if needed and garnish. Cheers! 

*if you prefer a slightly sweeter beverage
