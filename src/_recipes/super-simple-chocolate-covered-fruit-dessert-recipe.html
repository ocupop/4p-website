Tahini it may concern: Soom Chocolate Tahini is delicious, vegan, gluten-free, and from a woman-owned business. Dipping in season fruit in the chocolate tahini makes for a super simple dessert recipe. Depending on how much fruit and chocolate tahini you have, this recipe can serve 2 to however many people you like. At 4P Foods, we have fruit available year round including apples, fairtrade bananas, organic citrus, and berries. This sweet vegan snack is perfect for date night, movie night, or just a midnight treat.

ingredients_html: >-

Soom Chocolate Tahini

Your choice of in-season fruit

directions_html: >-

Spoon Soom Chocolate Tahini into a bowl. Stir the chocolate tahini to make sure it is smooth.

Slice larger pieces of fruit into bite sized pieces. Use forks to dip the fruit into the chocolate tahini. Happy eating!
