Caitlin is a passionate advocate for the success of local food systems. With over a decade of experience in the field of food and agriculture, she has held various roles, including managing sustainability programs in university dining services and overseeing the operations of farms and farm stores in the region. Caitlin derives immense joy from connecting the community of customers she serves with local and regenerative agricultural practices.
