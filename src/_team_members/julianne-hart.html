Julianne grew up in Tazewell, VA, on her family's farm, where they have cattle and horses. Growing up with access to the outdoors gave her a deep appreciation for nature. She love's to hike, forage, take care of plants, and ride horses. Graduate of Emory & Henry College, Julianne majored in Mass Communications and minored in Graphic Design. She read "Animal, Vegetable, Miracle" by Barbara Kingsolver in college and became interested in where her food comes from and how it is grown. Working at 4P has allowed her to bring together her work, interests, and values.
