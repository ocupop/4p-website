Lucie is an invaluable member of our Procurement Team here at 4P Foods! Hailing from the heart of DC, Lucie's passion for local farming and sustainable agriculture runs deep. Her years of hands-on work on small farms in the Northeast and studies in food and agriculture have fueled her unwavering drive to understand and improve our food system. Beyond her important work at 4P Foods, Lucie has an insatiable appetite for exploring the local food scene in the DMV, experimenting in the kitchen, and playing Ultimate Frisbee.
