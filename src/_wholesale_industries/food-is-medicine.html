4P Foods partners with healthcare providers in the Mid-Atlantic to deliver locally-grown produce prescription boxes and medically tailored groceries. These fruits and vegetables come from our network of more than 200 local farmers using planet-friendly growing practices.

Partnering with 4P Foods:

  • Ensures a nutritious supply of local fruits and vegetables.

  • Supports local farmers and the regional rural economy.

  • Tracks impact metrics over time.
