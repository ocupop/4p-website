Here is how our existing Food Is Medicine programs work:

Eligible patients are prescribed prescription boxes and medically tailored groceries by a Registered Dietitian Nutritionists (RDNs) or other Clinicians. Then, 4P Foods fulfills the prescriptions by delivering local food directly to the patients’ homes or community clinics.

Individuals and families will be provided, as appropriate, choice of healthy fruits and vegetables including culturally appropriate foods as well as opportunities to learn more about their local farmers.
