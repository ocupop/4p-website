Meet Our Farmers + Makers

We <3 our local farmers in Washington, D.C., Virginia, Maryland, and the Eastern Seaboard and Mid-Atlantic

At 4P Foods, rebuilding a regenerative and equitable food systems begins with working with farmers who are good stewards of land, food, animals, and communities. Our entire food system begins with them - the individual taking care of our lands and animals.

Our growing list of partners includes more than 200 farmers who value ethical and planet-friendly agricultural practices to provide local, sustainable, high-quality food year-round. Every month, we deliver freshly harvested produce, dairy, proteins, and pantry goods directly from these incredible humans - right to your doorstep.

Meet your local farmer right here at 4PFoods.com - and make them your food community too!
