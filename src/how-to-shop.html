The veggies and fruit so far have been amazing and I love the recipes in the newsletter. name: Jamie source: Facebook how_to_shop_guide: - title: Sign Up (or Log In) description_html: >-

If you're brand new, choose pickup or delivery, and please include delivery directions (clear and concise are always appreciated!). Enter your contact and billing information and you're ready to go shopping.

- title: Add 4P Bags to your cart description_html: >-

Produce, Protein, Dairy, and Fruit bags are available.

Make sure to select a recurring order if you want to get Good Food on a regular basis! Don't worry, you'll get reminders before each delivery so you don't forget that you've got food coming.

- title: Customize your 4P bags description_html: >-

Each 4P Bag comes "pre-packed" with 4P's Picks of the week based on what's fresh from our farmers and artisans. You'll receive a newsletter on Saturday afternoons letting you know that the week's fresh harvests are in and it's time to customize your Bags. 

The initial price of each standard Bag is consistent every week, and the final price will vary based on how you customize your Bags. Info on each Bag's starting price and minimum price can be found in the shop.

- title: Go shopping for a la carte items description_html: >-

Add on exactly what you need (extra produce when you have guests in town, eggs to your produce bag, your favorite local granola) or try out something new from a talented artisan producer.

Try building your own fully a la carte bag by adding individual items to your cart—as long as you hit the $30 minimum order, you're good to go.
