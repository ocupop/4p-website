--- layout: default title: How to Shop featured_image: /uploads/2020-10-09-4p-largebag-herdventures.jpg video_embed: how_to_shop_guide: - title: Sign Up (or Log In) description_html: >-

If you're brand new, choose pickup or delivery, and please include delivery directions (clear and concise are always appreciated!). Enter your contact and billing information and you're ready to go shopping.

If you're here for the first time from our old site, click the "Forgot your password" link on the login page to set up a new password.

- title: Add 4P Bags to your cart description_html: >-

Produce, protein, dairy, and fruit bags are available.

Make sure to select a recurring order if you want to get Good Food on a regular basis! Don't worry, you'll get reminders before each delivery so you don't forget that you've got food coming.

- title: Customize your 4P bags description_html: >-

Produce and new fruit bags have a set price and number of items within each size. Swap out any item you don't want from our weekly picks and then add in any swap item you do want, one for one.

Protein and dairy bags have flexible prices based on what you put in them. We pack a "standard" bag with our 4P picks each week, and you have the freedom to add or take away as much as you want, with a minimum price of $20 per bag.

- title: Go shopping for a la carte items description_html: >-

Add on exactly what you need (extra produce when you have guests in town, eggs to your produce bag, your favorite local granola) or try out something new from a talented artisan producer.

Try building your own fully a la carte bag by adding individual items to your cart—as long as you hit the $30 minimum order, you're good to go. And all orders $50 and up get free delivery!
