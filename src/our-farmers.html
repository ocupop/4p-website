Meet our farmers

We are your direct connection to the farm

4P Foods sources fresh, high-quality, delicious food, prioritizing local and regional partners. We cultivate a network of growers and producers who engage in labor practices that enable people and communities to thrive, and in growing practices that actively build the soil and promote biodiversity. Year-round, you can get freshly harvested produce, dairy and meat products delivered directly from our trusted network of farms and artisans.
