Good Food With a Purpose

We define “Good Food” as more than just local. To us, good food is: transparently sourced so you know where it came from, healthy to nourish both body and mind, ethically raised with no souls exploited in its production, and harvested in a way that maintains the health of the environment. Most of all, Good Food is equitably accessible to all people, regardless of race, income, gender, or zipcode.

4P Foods is part of the rapidly-growing Good Food Movement that is building a better food system focused on equity, health, long-term sustainability, ethical and transparent sourcing.
