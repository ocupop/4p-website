Good Food With a Purpose

We define “Good Food” as more than just local. To us, good food: provides nourishment and enables people to thrive, is equitably accessible to everyone in the region, is produced in a manner that is environmentally regenerative, comes from a supply chain in which every being is treated with respect and dignity, and improves health outcomes and economic security for all.

4P Foods is part of the rapidly-growing Good Food Movement that is building a better food system focused on equity, health, long-term sustainability, and ethical and transparent sourcing.
