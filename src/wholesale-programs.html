Wholesale Programs

Good Food for Your Business

We know that local sourcing can be challenging for businesses, and that consumers increasingly want to know where their food comes from and how it was grown. We are here to help!

Our extensive wholesale offerings include produce, meat, dairy, pantry, and value-added grocery items, all source-identified so you know exactly where they come from. We work with farmers and buyers to make the process as easy and successful as possible on both sides.
